Perfectly Chocolate Chocolate Cake

Moist cocoa layers + a silky chocolate frosting. A classic, no-fuss celebration cake.

Yield: 1 (2-layer) 9-inch cake Prep: 25 min Bake: 30–35 min Total: ~1 hr 20 min Oven: 350°F / 175°C

Ingredients (Cake)

  • 2 cups (250 g) all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups (400 g) granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup (75 g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp fine salt
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 cup (240 ml) whole milk (or buttermilk)
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) neutral oil (canola/vegetable)
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240 ml) hot coffee or hot water

Ingredients (Chocolate Frosting)

  • 1 cup (225 g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 1/2 cups (420 g) powdered sugar
  • 3/4 cup (75 g) cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) milk or cream, plus more as needed

Note: Hot coffee deepens the chocolate flavor (it won’t taste like coffee). Hot water works too.

Steps

  1. Prep pans: Heat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease two 9-inch (23 cm) round pans, line bottoms with parchment, then lightly flour the sides.
  2. Mix dry: In a large bowl, whisk flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt until evenly combined.
  3. Add wet: Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Mix until smooth (about 1 minute). Scrape down the bowl.
  4. Thin the batter: With mixer on low, slowly pour in hot coffee (or hot water). Batter will be thin—this is normal.
  5. Bake: Divide batter evenly between pans. Bake 30–35 minutes, until a toothpick comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  6. Cool: Cool in pans 10 minutes. Turn out onto a rack and cool completely before frosting.
  7. Make frosting: Beat butter 2 minutes until fluffy. Add powdered sugar, cocoa, and salt; beat on low to combine. Add vanilla and milk/cream; beat 2–3 minutes until smooth and spreadable. Add more milk/cream 1 tbsp at a time if needed.
  8. Assemble: Place one layer on a plate, spread frosting on top, add second layer, then frost top and sides. Slice and serve.

Optional: For extra neat layers, chill the cakes 20–30 minutes before frosting.

Tip: Store covered at room temperature for up to 2 days, or refrigerate up to 5 days. Bring to room temp before serving for best texture.
